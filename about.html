What does “Project C190” mean?

At age 9, I started messing around on kids' programming website Scratch, publishing my work under the username Crafty190. Before I knew it, I went from making Warrior Cats RPGs in blocks to dabbling in stuff like assembly language, and now I'm studying computer science in college. This website is named "Project C190" after "Crafty190" as a tribute to how my time on Scratch has shaped my interests. Also, fun fact, many of the graphics are drawn in Scratch with a mouse!
