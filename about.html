What does “Project C190” mean?

At age 9, I started messing around on kids' programming website Scratch, publishing my work under the username C_____190. Before I knew it, I went from making Warrior Cats RPGs in blocks to dabbling in stuff like assembly language, and now I'm studying computer science in university. This website is named "Project C190" as a tribute to how my time on Scratch has shaped my interests. Also, fun fact, some of the graphics are drawn in Scratch with a mouse just as I would have done as a kid.
